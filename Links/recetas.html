  • Meet-balls without gluten.
    • Ingredients:

      • 350 g of ground beef

      350 g of ground pork

      1 small onion, finely chopped

      2 cloves garlic, finely chopped

      2 g (1/2 teaspoon) of salt

      15 g (1 tbsp) fresh basil or 2 ml (1/2 tsp) dried basil, chopped

      1 egg, beaten

      750 g of crushed Corn Flakes cereal Gluten NESTLE Sin

    • Steps:

      • Heat oven to 190 C. Prepare a pan of 29 x 27 x 2.5 cm with foil; Grease the foil with cooking spray.

      In a small bowl, mix beef and ground pork with onion, garlic, salt, basil, egg and 15 g of crushed corn flakes. Form 48 balls with the mixture of 2.5 cm.

      Pass the dough balls on the remaining corn flakes. Locate the meatballs on the skillet.

      Bake for 18-25 minutes or until no pink in the center. Serve with ketchup.
