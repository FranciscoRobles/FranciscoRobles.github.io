Problem:

Recent medical studies have shown that 1 in 100 people may have celiac disease or gluten intolerance. This disease is a disorder of small intestine. Some of the symptoms are:

  • Diarrhea.
  • Malnutrition.
  • Intestinal cramps.

The biggest problem when someone is diagnosed with this disease, is that you do not know exactly where to buy gluten-free foods, or what restaurants you can go that offer gluten-free meal.

Audience:

Our product is aimed at people who are intolerant to gluten.

Solution and Impact:

Our solution is to create an app that can tell the user the food he/she can eat, in which markets he/she can find gluten-free food and the name of the restaurants that have gluten-free meals.It will have an important impact in making easier the life of the people that have this disease.
